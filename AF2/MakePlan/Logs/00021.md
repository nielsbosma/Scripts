### Research Findings

**Ivy.Analyser** exists at `D:\Repos\_Ivy\Ivy-Framework\src\Ivy.Analyser\` with 5 "Rules of Hooks" diagnostics:

| Rule | Severity | Description |
|------|----------|-------------|
| IVYHOOK001 | Error | Hooks called inside lambdas, local functions, or other methods |
| IVYHOOK002 | Warning | Hooks called conditionally (if/ternary) |
| IVYHOOK003 | Warning | Hooks called in loops |
| IVYHOOK004 | Warning | Hooks called in switch statements |
| IVYHOOK005 | Warning | Hooks not at top of Build() method |

**Current state:** Published to NuGet (v1.0.0/1.0.1/1.0.48) but NOT referenced by any production project. The reference in Ivy.Samples.Shared is commented out. New projects via `ivy init` don't include it.

**Plan:** Modify `Project.tpl` template to auto-include analyzer + uncomment in Samples.
